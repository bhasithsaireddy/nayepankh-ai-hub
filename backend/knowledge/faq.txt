FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT NAYEPANKH FOUNDATION

Q: How can I volunteer with NayePankh Foundation?
A: You can join NayePankh by visiting nayepankh.com and clicking Join Us or Donate. 
You can contribute your time, skills, or money. No prior experience is needed. 
Students from all backgrounds are welcome.

Q: What departments can I volunteer in?
A: NayePankh has five departments you can join based on your skills:
- Education: for those who like teaching, tutoring, or creating content
- Food Relief: for those who can help with logistics or distribution
- Women Empowerment: for those interested in healthcare or social work
- Tech and Media: for those with skills in coding, design, or social media
- Fundraising: for those good at communication, marketing, or outreach

Q: How can I donate to NayePankh Foundation?
A: Donations can be made on the website at nayepankh.com/donate. All donations 
are tax exempt under Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act. You can donate 
money, clothes, food, or your skills and time.

Q: Is NayePankh Foundation legitimate?
A: Yes. It is registered with the Uttar Pradesh Government under the Societies 
Registration Act and holds 12A and 80G certifications from the Indian government.

Q: Does NayePankh offer internships?
A: Yes. NayePankh runs structured one-month remote internships for students 
across roles including web development, AI, machine learning, Python, Java, 
UI/UX, data analytics, mobile development, and more. Internships involve 
weekly tasks and real project work.

Q: Where does NayePankh operate?
A: Currently active in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities in 
Uttar Pradesh, India. Remote volunteering and internships are also available.
