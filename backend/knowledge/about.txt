NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led, non-governmental, non-profit organization 
registered under the Societies Registration Act with the Uttar Pradesh Government.

It holds 12A and 12G certifications which means donations are tax exempt under 
Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act.

The organization was founded during the COVID-19 pandemic around 2020 when a group 
of high school students began helping underprivileged people with whatever resources 
they could arrange.

It officially became a formal NGO on 28 March 2021.

NayePankh describes itself as one of the biggest student-led NGOs in India.

Operations are currently active in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities 
across Uttar Pradesh.

The founder and president is Prashant Shukla.
