NayePankh Foundation works across five core mission areas:

1. EDUCATION
Providing quality education to underprivileged children through fun and engaging 
activities. Running awareness campaigns in schools and communities. Supporting 
children who cannot afford formal schooling.

2. FOOD DISTRIBUTION
Distributing food to underprivileged communities and stray animals as part of 
hunger relief efforts. Running regular food drives in Kanpur and Ghaziabad.

3. MENSTRUAL HYGIENE AND WOMEN EMPOWERMENT
Distributing sanitary napkins to women and girls who cannot afford them.
Running awareness drives so women feel less humiliated during menstruation 
in public spaces. Empowering women through education and outreach.

4. CLOTHES DISTRIBUTION
Supplying clothes to poor families as part of broader welfare drives especially 
during winter months.

5. STRAY ANIMAL SUPPORT
Providing food and care to stray animals as part of their compassion-driven 
community outreach.

The organization believes service to mankind is service to god and aims to 
revolutionize society one person at a time.
